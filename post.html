2026 Scheduling

Nov 20 2025 2 Min read

The season kicks off with regional “Kickoff” tournaments starting January 15 – February 15, 2026, with different regions beginning on slightly different days (Americas on Jan 15, EMEA on Jan 20, Pacific and China on Jan 22).

Following Masters Santiago is Stage 1, running from March 31 – May 24, 2026, with regional leagues competing in a group-to-playoff format. Then the second international event, VALORANT Masters London 2026, takes place June 6 – June 21, 2026, in London — the first time the UK will host a Masters event.

After that: Stage 2 runs June 30 – August 16, 2026, then teams proceed to the Season Finals in early September, culminating in the year-ending VALORANT Champions 2026 in Shanghai, China — set for September 24 – October 18, 2026.
