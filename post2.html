NRG sign Keiko for 2026

Nov 12 2025 4 Min read

In a significant offseason move, NRG has officially signed former Team Liquid star keiko, bringing him into the lineup as they prepare to defend their crown from the VALORANT Champions 2025. The signing was announced on November 13, 2025, filling the vacancy left by long-time player Sam "s0m" Oh, who recently stepped away from competition. With keiko onboard, NRG’s 2026 roster now includes Ethan, mada, skuba, brawk, and keiko — a squad that blends experience and fresh talent.

Keiko arrives having built a strong reputation in the European region. During his 2025 season with Team Liquid, he helped secure the EMEA Stage 2 title, contributing as one of the region’s most reliable controllers and utility players. Despite Team Liquid’s underwhelming international results, keiko distinguished himself with high individual performance metrics — a fact not lost on NRG as they looked for someone to match s0m’s impact. Many observers are already calling the move “a perfect pickup,” noting keiko’s versatility and potential to add firepower to an already championship-ready roster.
